Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Chef Yogesh Kumar, our restaurant began as a small family-owned eatery with a passion for authentic flavors and traditional recipes. Over the years, we have grown into one of the region's premier dining destinations while maintaining our commitment to quality and hospitality.

With a diverse menu that celebrates both vegetarian and non-vegetarian cuisines, we take pride in sourcing the freshest ingredients from local farmers and fishermen. Our specialty seafood dishes have earned us recognition throughout the region, with our signature fish curries and grilled specialties drawing food enthusiasts from far and wide.

At Yogesh Hotel, we believe that great food brings people together. Whether you're joining us for a casual meal with friends, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, our team is dedicated to providing you with an unforgettable dining experience.

Authentic Cuisine

Our chefs prepare dishes using traditional recipes and techniques passed down through generations, ensuring an authentic culinary experience.

Fresh Seafood

We source our seafood daily from local fishermen, guaranteeing the freshest catch for our specialty fish and seafood dishes.

Vegetarian Delights

Our extensive vegetarian menu offers innovative and flavorful dishes that showcase the versatility of plant-based ingredients.

Our Journey

From a small family restaurant to a culinary destination

2005

The Beginning

Yogesh Hotel opened its doors as a small 20-seat restaurant focusing on traditional home-style cooking.

2010

Expansion

Relocated to our current location with expanded seating and introduced our now-famous seafood menu.

2015

Recognition

Received the "Best Seafood Restaurant" award and expanded our menu to include more regional specialties.

2020

Innovation

Launched our signature chef's tasting menu featuring the best of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian cuisines.

Today

Continuing the Tradition

We continue to serve delicious food with the same passion and dedication as day one, while constantly innovating and perfecting our craft.
